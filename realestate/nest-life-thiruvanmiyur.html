Property Description

Nest Life by Nest Builders at Thiruvanmiyur is designed specifically for those who wish to live and have a complete independent feeling in a “flats system”. Located just one kilometer away from the Thiruvanmiyur beach, Nest Life offers premium Luxurious 3 BHK apartments with a small commercial space in the Ground floor. Nest Life consists of two floors, one apartment in each that is designed meticulously with tremendous all-side ventilations. Nest Life’s location has access to beaches, a pollution-free environment, excellent hang-out spots, malls, offices, hospitals, et cetera.

Nest Builders have come up with another wonderful construction of lavish and spacious flats i.e, Project “Nest Life”, which is located in the most promising area at Thiruvanmiyur. The project brings best living qualities and conditions and is designed meticulously for people who exclusively value privacy and independency over other things. The project consists of only two apartments totally, which gives an almost independent house feeling, with an apartment outlook. The standards of construction and luxurious amenities and lifestyle involved is sure to dazzle people and hence makes it one of the most urban places to live in.

Nest Builders’ “Nest life” is another great construction and a culmination of sophistication, stylishness, airy & spacious rooms with boundless expediency as well as the best of facilities.
