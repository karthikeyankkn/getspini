Property Description

"With an eclectic mix of aesthetics, liveliness and style, Navin’s Starwood Towers 2.0 is designed by Navin Housing and Properties to offer extreme happiness and joy to residents. Located in Vengaivasal, Chennai, this project offers ample amenities to meet the requirements of residents. The amenities offered are, a gymnasium, swimming pool, children’s play area, clubhouse, rainwater harvesting, intercom, 24 X 7 security, power backup, landscaped gardens, indoor games, maintenance staff, community hall, basketball court, tennis court, library, health facilities, badminton court etc. Navin’s Starwood Towers 2.0 lies close to Ravindra Bharathi Global School, T.I.M.E. Kids Preschool, Oral Health Care Center, V Care Clinic and Shopping Singapore. Navin’s Starwood Towers 2.0 is strategically located off Grand Southern Trunk (GST) Road that provides direct connectivity to key areas of the city like Medavakkam, Sholinganallur, Selaiyur, Pallikaranai etc. These exquisitely crafted apartments will provide for an eminent living destination for you and your family members."
