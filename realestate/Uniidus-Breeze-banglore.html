Property Description

Uniidus Breeze is Boutique Apartment in Budget price, Total 120 Flats of 1, 2 & 3BHK in Munnekolallu Road ( Marathahalli), Next to Vagdevi Vilas School (CBSE Affiliated till 12th class, One of Most Sought After School in the Area). Uniidus Breeze is Approved from BBMP, Permission to get A Khata, “CC” (Completion Certificate) and “OC” (Occupancy Certificate) will be provided. “RERA” Approved Project.

Bank Loans- Project Approved from all Major Banks Including SBI. More than 5 Different Connectivities to the Project. 1.5KM distance from Outer Ring Road, 1.6 KM from Varthur Road, Just 500 Mtrs from Nearest Bus Stop. 50Mtrs from Vagdevi Vilas School.

Uniidus Breeze is well surrounded by all IT Parks/ Schools/ Hospitals/ Several Colleges / Schools/ Shops for Daily needs etc. Area is densely populated and hence clean land is scarce and thus creates a high demand factor.

Highlights : Vaastu Compliant, Covered Car Parking, Earthquake Resistant Design, Exterior Textured Paints, High End Bathroom Fittings, Designer Common Passages with Granite Flooring and False Ceilings.

Amenities : Swimming Pool with Kids Pool, Gym, Multi Purpose Party Hall, Basket Ball Hoop, Cricket Pitch, Table Tennis, Children play Area with Play equipments for infants , shuttle area, Sit out for elders, Herb landscape garden, bird bath, water feature, Solar lighting in Common area and Drive way area etc. Fire safety equipment/ Cauvery water and bore well connection, CCTV, Intercom, STP, Generator Back Up, Rain Water Re-Cycling.
