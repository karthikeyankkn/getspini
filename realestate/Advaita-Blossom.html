Property Description

"Advaita Blossom is a residential project by Advaita Homes, located in Kelambakkam, Chennai. It is an under construction project and its beautiful and spacious layout plan includes 356 units and 6 towers in total.

Advaita Blossom will have smart affordable flats for the convenience of the people. Advaita Blossom floor plan has 1 BHK, 2 BHK and 3 BHK flats. The price of the area ranges from 25.0 Lac - 53.2 Lac (3,662 per sqft - 3,834 per sqft). The kitchen, living, dining room and bedroom floorings will have high- quality of vitrified tiles. The balcony and toilet floorings will have premium anti-skid ceramic tiles. The exterior and interior walls will either have POP or gypsum finished plaster. The kitchen and toilet walls will have good quality designed dado tiles. All electrical wiring in the residential area will be concealed with high-quality PVC conduit pipes. You will be provided with stainless steel sink in the kitchen area. The apartment will have designer toilets and bathrooms. Flats will have UPVC sliding windows with or without provision for the grill as per the architect design.

Advaita Blossom will include club house, jogging and strolling track and private terrace or garden to not only beautify the complex, but also to help you maintain your health.

Apartment will provide all the basic facilities including power back up, lift, security, reserved parking, parking for visitors, intercom facility, staff for maintenance, banquet hall, fitness centre, DTH television facility, water storage and installment of fire fighting equipments so that you do not face any issues in your day to day life.

Advaita Blossom will give emphasis on self-care that is why will provide an indoor games room, kids play area, indoor squash courts, badminton courts, multipurpose courts, meditation room and early learning centre for kids.

Highlights

Advaita Blossom Chennai is in close proximity to hospital, shopping complex, schools, bus station, Atms, pharmacy, grocery stores, food joints, movie halls, gardens, petrol pumps and gyms. St Mary's Matriculation Higher Secondary School is at the distance of 0.8 km from the apartment and it takes only 5 minutes to reach there by car. Advaita Blossom photos are available online at magicbricks website."
