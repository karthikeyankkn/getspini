Property Description

"Appaswamy Cerus is a project by Appaswamy Real Estate located in Virugambakkam Chennai. It is build on an area of 2 Acre encompassing 166 units across 3 towers. The floorings are made of Vitrified Tiles 3x3, Wooden Flooring and Anti Skid Tiles. Appaswamy Cerus comprises of 2BHK, 3BHK, 4BHK and 5BHK House, Flat and Penthouse. 2BHK House has area 18664 Sq-ft having balcony and 10 bathroom. 5BHK Flat has a range of area from 3135 Sq-ft to 3270 Sq-ft having 2 balcony and 4 bathroom. 4BHK Penthouse has area 3111 Sq-ft having 2 balcony and 4 bathroom. 2BHK Flat has a range of area from 2829 Sq-ft to 3111 Sq-ft . 3BHK Flat has a range of area from 1709 Sq-ft to 3212 Sq-ft having 1 and 2 balcony and 3 bathroom. Launched in May 2011, Appaswamy Cerus offers Ready To Move properties. It is approved by various banks like HDFC, ICICI, LIC Housing Finance and SBI. The major Amenities in the project are Park, Large Open space and Solar Energy. Lift, Banquet Hall, Located in the Heart of city and Multipurpose Hall. Indoor Squash & Badminton Courts, AEROBICS ROOM and Health club with Steam / Jaccuzi. Private Terrace/Garden, Mini Cinema Theatre and Large Clubhouse. "
