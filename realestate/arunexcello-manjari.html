Property Description

On the sustained demand from a larger section of the society, we are proud to announce the launch of our “Tenth Project” under “Compact Homes” series in the name & style of “Manjari”.

The apartments in offer are 208 functional units with efficient design and crisp elevation with no frills attached. This will cater to a large cross section of the society who can now hope to own a dream home which will be within their reach.

This project “Manjari” is located in Irungattukottai, close to several MNCs on NH 4 (Bengaluru Highway), Reputed Hospitals, Prominent Schools & Colleges, Theme Parks, the proposed International Airport and one of the fast developing corridors with good road connectivity and other social infrastructure. This project is laid over 1.80 acres of land with landscaped area and Other Facilities.

These Compact Homes are offered at an affordable and competitive price. The apartment size ranges from 595 sft to 635 sft. All of them are 2 BHK in a Ground plus 3 Floors structure.
