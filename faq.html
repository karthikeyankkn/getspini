FAQ

DEFINITION OF TERMS

What is a lead?

Any information that leads to a purchase by someone is a lead.

Examples of Leads:

  • You want to buy a new car.
  • Your Neighbourhood shopkeeper wants a small cash loan.
  • You know someone who is looking for a property to buy or rent.
  • You know a friend who is looking for a life or healthcare insurance policy.

Who are the Referrers?

Anyone who posts business opportunity information about buyers on Spini.

Who are the Buyers?

Anyone who.s interested in purchasing or getting any form of product or service.

Who are the Salespeople?

Anyone whose profession involves selling of products and services. For example, insurance agents, property agents and travel agents.

Who are the Community Managers?

Community Managers are Spini.s agents. They will be there to assist you and will be there to manage the marketplace. This includes the verification of leads.

What is a Sales Assists?

A sales assist is the contact information of the buyer that is provided by the referrer.

Why should I post information I know on Spini?

Because every piece of information has value and Spini helps you to monetize it through a marketplace. Certain information may not be directly useful to you or your business. However, it might be useful to someone else. Therefore, sell it to them and help them succeed. In return, you get to make some money.

Why would anyone pay money for the information that I have?

There are many forms of Salespeople. They could be selling something related to the information that you have. For example, you know that your friend.s company might be looking to buy new computers. While this is purely information to you, it is a sales lead to computer sellers. Therefore, your lead could be critical information to Salespeople. Spini helps you connect and monetize that information. Therefore, Salespeople would be motivated to pay for leads as it could lead to a sales opportunity.

Will posting my friend's personal information on Spini lead to feelings of animosity between the two of us? Especially when I made some money out of it.

Posting a lead on Spini is anonymous. No one would know who the poster is. However, you are also welcome to post leads that do not have an agent fee. Therefore, you would not be earning anything but helping both your friend and the Salesperson. You could also invite your friend to Spini so that he too can make money by selling leads! There is a possibility of a bonus of an advance discount. However, if money is important for you, all lead posts will be anonymous and will not reveal your true identity. No one would know who posted.
